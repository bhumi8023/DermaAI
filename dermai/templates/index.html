        The Technology

        How DermAI Works

        01
        Image Capture

        You upload a facial photo. OpenCV detects your face using Haar Cascade classifiers and isolates the skin region.

        02
        Pixel Analysis

        The RGB, HSV, and LAB color spaces are analyzed to quantify redness, oiliness, texture roughness, dark spots, and moisture levels.

        03
        Environment Correlation

        Real-time PM2.5, O₃, and NO₂ from OpenWeatherMap are fetched for your city and correlated with skin oxidative stress models.

        04
        Holistic Scoring

        Sleep deprivation, hormonal phase, and stress are weighted against skin metrics to compute a composite health score and tailored recommendations.
